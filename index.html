5 Day Week for bank employees

this is not just a demand, this is a necessity


Current status: The standard work week for banks in India remains 5 days, with the second and fourth Saturdays off.
Employee proposals: Employee unions have been advocating for a full 5-day work week, citing potential benefits like increased productivity and reduced stress, but these proposals are still pending implementation.
Official clarification: The RBI has officially stated that it has not issued any instructions for a 5-day week and that such reports are incorrect.
Implementation pending: Despite the agreement between the Indian Banks' Association (IBA) and employee unions to recommend a 5-day work week, the implementation has not yet taken place.
